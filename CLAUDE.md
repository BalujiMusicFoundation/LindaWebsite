## About Linda Shanson
- Also known as **Linda Shanovitch**
- Singer, songwriter, dancer, poet, anthropologist (BSc Anthropology)
- Lived and worked in **Mexico, India, Paris**
- First song *Winter in Spring* published by Northern Songs, broadcast on **BBC aged 13**
- Vocalist of **Jazz Orient / Re-Orient** with husband **Baluji Shrivastav OBE** (blind sitar master — worked with Stevie Wonder, Annie Lennox, Shakira)
- 6+ albums on **ARC Music / Naxos**: *Bird Dancer*, *Dangerous Ground*, *Live at the South Bank*, *Undiscovered Time* (2012), *Sangeet* (single 2022)
- Co-founded **Baluji Music Foundation** (2008) — registered UK charity
- Manager of the **Inner Vision Orchestra** — world's only professional ensemble of blind musicians (18 members)
- Composed the **2023 IBSA World Games Anthem** — performed at Symphony Hall, Birmingham
- Inner Vision Orchestra performed at **London 2012 Paralympics closing ceremony** with Coldplay
- Headlined **2023 EFG London Jazz Festival**
- Author of **Frankenstein in India** (Sampson Low, 2018)
- Contact: lindashanson1@hotmail.com
- Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/lindashansonartist/
- Streaming: https://jazzorient.bandcamp.com / Apple Music / Deezer / Discogs
